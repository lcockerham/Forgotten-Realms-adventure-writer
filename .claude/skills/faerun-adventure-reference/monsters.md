# Faerun Monster Reference

Quick reference for selecting level-appropriate monsters for Faerun adventures. Use this to build balanced encounters and ensure regional accuracy.

## How to Use This Reference

1. **Find party level** in the XP Budget table below
2. **Choose difficulty** (Low/Moderate/High)
3. **Calculate XP budget** = Budget per character × 4 (standard party)
4. **Select monsters** from the lists below, spending XP until budget is used

## Quick Reference: Monsters by Level Tier

### Tier 1 (Levels 1-4) - Local Heroes
Use CR 0-2 creatures. Budget: 200-2,000 XP per encounter.
- **Common**: Goblins, kobolds, bandits, skeletons, zombies
- **Leaders**: Bugbear, ghast, ogre, cult fanatic
- **Bosses**: Wight (CR 3), green hag (CR 3)

### Tier 2 (Levels 5-10) - Heroes of the Realm
Use CR 3-8 creatures. Budget: 2,000-12,000 XP per encounter.
- **Common**: Ogres, trolls, elementals, vampire spawn
- **Leaders**: Young dragons, mind flayers, drow mage
- **Bosses**: Hydra (CR 8), frost giant (CR 8)

### Tier 3 (Levels 11-16) - Masters of the Realm
Use CR 9-15 creatures. Budget: 8,000-40,000 XP per encounter.
- **Common**: Giants, demons, devils, aboleths
- **Leaders**: Adult dragons, beholders, vampire
- **Bosses**: Mummy lord (CR 15), purple worm (CR 15)

### Tier 4 (Levels 17-20) - Masters of the World
Use CR 16+ creatures. Budget: 30,000+ XP per encounter.
- **Common**: Adult dragons, mariliths, pit fiends
- **Leaders**: Ancient dragons, liches
- **Bosses**: Tarrasque (CR 30)

## Encounter Building Examples

### Level 3 Party (4 PCs), Moderate Difficulty
- Budget: 225 × 4 = 900 XP
- Options:
  - 1 Bugbear (200) + 4 Goblins (200) = 400 XP (under budget, easy-moderate)
  - 2 Ghouls (400) + 2 Skeletons (100) = 500 XP
  - 1 Wight (700 XP) = 700 XP (near budget, challenging)

### Level 7 Party (4 PCs), High Difficulty
- Budget: 1,700 × 4 = 6,800 XP
- Options:
  - 1 Young Black Dragon (2,900) + 2 Giant Crocodiles (900) = 3,800 XP
  - 1 Mind Flayer (2,900) + 4 Gricks (900) = 3,800 XP
  - 2 Trolls (3,600) + 3 Ogres (1,350) = 4,950 XP

### Level 12 Party (4 PCs), Moderate Difficulty
- Budget: 3,700 × 4 = 14,800 XP
- Options:
  - 1 Adult Black Dragon (11,500) + 2 Wyverns (4,600) = 16,100 XP (slightly over)
  - 1 Beholder (10,000) + 4 Spectators (2,800) = 12,800 XP
  - 2 Bone Devils (10,000) + 4 Imps (800) = 10,800 XP

## Validation Notes

When selecting monsters for adventures:
1. **Check CR vs Level**: Monster CR should roughly match party level for solo encounters
2. **Calculate XP Budget**: Never exceed High difficulty budget without story justification
3. **Verify Regional Fit**: Don't put ice creatures in Calimshan or fire creatures in Icewind Dale
4. **Check Source**: All monsters should be from official sources (MM, VGM, MTF)
5. **Consider Action Economy**: 4+ enemies of appropriate CR can be deadlier than CR suggests
