# Faerun Deities Reference

Detailed deity information for creating authentic Faerun adventures. Use these details to add depth, faction connections, and thematic hooks.

## How to Use This Reference

- **Specialty Priests**: Unique titles for devoted clergy (great for NPC names)
- **Major Temples**: Specific locations for adventure settings
- **Allies**: Deities/faiths that cooperate (for multi-faction hooks)
- **Foes**: Opposing deities (for conflict-driven adventures)

## Good-Aligned Deities

### Tymora (Lady Luck)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Good
- **Domains**: Trickery
- **Portfolio**: Luck, good fortune, adventurers, skill
- **Symbol**: Silver coin with Tymora's face
- **Specialty Priests**: Luckbringers
- **Major Temples**:
  - Temple of Lady Luck (Arabel, Cormyr)
  - House of the Lady (Waterdeep, Dock Ward)
  - Ladyshall (Shadowdale)
- **Allies**: Lathander, Selûne, Sune, good adventuring deities
- **Foes**: Beshaba (her dark twin), Bane, Moander
- **Common Followers**: Adventurers, gamblers, rogues, sailors, travelers
- **Adventure Hooks**: Cursed gambling dens, thieves seeking divine luck, competitions blessed by Tymora

### Lathander (The Morninglord)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Life, Light
- **Portfolio**: Dawn, renewal, birth, creativity, youth, athletics
- **Symbol**: Road traveling into sunrise
- **Specialty Priests**: Awakened, Dawnbringers
- **Major Temples**:
  - Spires of the Morning (Waterdeep, Sea Ward)
  - The Towers of the Morning (Telpir, Returned Abeir)
  - Lathander's Light (Beregost)
- **Allies**: Chauntea, Sune, Tymora, Gond, Selûne
- **Foes**: Cyric, Talos, undead deities (Myrkul, Velsharoon), Shar
- **Common Followers**: Nobles, artists, athletes, spring festivals, paladins
- **Adventure Hooks**: Renewal rituals gone wrong, undead threats to dawn temples, corrupt nobles seeking rebirth

### Torm (The Loyal Fury)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Domains**: War, Order
- **Portfolio**: Duty, loyalty, obedience, paladins, righteousness
- **Symbol**: White gauntlet on blue field
- **Specialty Priests**: Champions, Holy Champions
- **Major Temples**:
  - Halls of Valor (Tantras)
  - Temple Loyal Fury (Procampur)
  - Various strongholds of the Order of the Gauntlet
- **Allies**: Tyr, Ilmater, Lathander, Helm, Red Knight
- **Foes**: Bane, Cyric, Mask, Shar, all evil deities
- **Common Followers**: Paladins, soldiers, lawkeepers, bodyguards
- **Adventure Hooks**: Oathbreakers seeking redemption, corrupt city guards, protecting nobles from assassination

### Chauntea (The Great Mother)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Life, Nature
- **Portfolio**: Agriculture, plants cultivated by humans, farmers, gardeners, summer
- **Symbol**: Blooming rose on sunburst
- **Specialty Priests**: Harvesters
- **Major Temples**:
  - Goldenfields (near Waterdeep)
  - House of the Harvest (Waterdeep, Castle Ward)
  - Numerous small shrines in farming communities
- **Allies**: Lathander, Silvanus, Eldath, Mielikki, Lurue
- **Foes**: Talos, Malar, Talona, Moander, Auril
- **Common Followers**: Farmers, gardeners, herbalists, rural folk
- **Adventure Hooks**: Blighted crops, corrupted harvest festivals, drought curses, nature elementals

### Selûne (Our Lady of Silver)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Good
- **Domains**: Knowledge, Life, Twilight
- **Portfolio**: Moon, stars, navigation, prophecy, questers, lycanthropes (good)
- **Symbol**: Pair of eyes surrounded by seven silver stars
- **Specialty Priests**: Silverstars
- **Major Temples**:
  - House of the Moon (Waterdeep, Sea Ward)
  - Tower of the Moon (Tethyr)
  - Numerous moonstone circles
- **Allies**: Mystra, Lathander, Tymora, Sune, Eilistraee
- **Foes**: Shar (her twin sister and eternal enemy), Umberlee
- **Common Followers**: Navigators, sailors, moon elves, female spellcasters, good lycanthropes
- **Adventure Hooks**: Shar vs. Selûne conflicts, werewolf redemption, lunar prophecies, shadow corruption

### Ilmater (The Crying God)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Domains**: Life, Twilight
- **Portfolio**: Endurance, suffering, martyrdom, perseverance
- **Symbol**: Hands bound at wrist with red cord
- **Specialty Priests**: Painbearers, Adorned
- **Major Temples**:
  - Monastery of the Yellow Rose (Damara)
  - House of the Broken God (Keltar, Calimshan)
  - Numerous hospices and healing houses
- **Allies**: Tyr, Torm, Lathander, Eldath
- **Foes**: Loviatar, Talona, Malar, all who cause suffering for pleasure
- **Common Followers**: Healers, monks, torture victims, enslaved, martyrs
- **Adventure Hooks**: Slave liberation, torture cults, plague relief, protecting refugees

## Neutral Deities

### Mystra (The Lady of Mysteries)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Arcana, Knowledge
- **Portfolio**: Magic, spells, the Weave, magic items
- **Symbol**: Circle of seven stars, or nine stars encircling a flowing red mist
- **Specialty Priests**: Spellfire Wielders (rare)
- **Major Temples**:
  - House of Wonder (Waterdeep, Castle Ward - shared with Azuth)
  - Tower of Mysteries (Yhaunn, Sembia)
  - Various wizard academies
- **Allies**: Azuth (her chosen), Savras, Velsharoon (reluctantly), Kelemvor
- **Foes**: Cyric, Shar, those who would destroy magic
- **Common Followers**: Wizards, sorcerers, magic item crafters, scholars
- **Adventure Hooks**: Weave disturbances, stolen spellbooks, rogue magic items, Spellplague remnants

### Kelemvor (Lord of the Dead)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral
- **Domains**: Death, Grave
- **Portfolio**: Death, the dead, judgment of souls
- **Symbol**: Upright skeletal arm holding balanced scales
- **Specialty Priests**: Doomguides
- **Major Temples**:
  - Crypt of Kelemvor (Ormath, Sembia)
  - Eternal Rest (Waterdeep, City of the Dead)
  - Various crypts and mausoleums
- **Allies**: Mystra (former lover), Jergal (seneschal), Deneir
- **Foes**: Cyric, Velsharoon, Orcus, undead creators
- **Common Followers**: Grave diggers, morticians, judges, those who hunt undead
- **Adventure Hooks**: Undead outbreaks, souls trapped in Fugue Plane, necromancer cults, proper burials

### Silvanus (Oak Father)
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: Nature
- **Portfolio**: Wild nature, druids, forests
- **Symbol**: Oak leaf
- **Specialty Priests**: Hierophants
- **Major Temples**:
  - Tall Trees (Neverwinter Wood)
  - The Oakengrove Abbey (Turmish)
  - Sacred groves throughout Faerûn
- **Allies**: Mielikki, Eldath, Chauntea, Shiallia
- **Foes**: Malar, Talos, Talona, those who destroy nature
- **Common Followers**: Druids, rangers, woodsmen, rural communities
- **Adventure Hooks**: Logging conflicts, corrupted groves, elemental imbalances, awakened tree guardians

### Gond (Wonderbringer)
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: Knowledge, Forge
- **Portfolio**: Artifice, craft, construction, smithwork
- **Symbol**: Toothed cog with four spokes
- **Specialty Priests**: Imagineers
- **Major Temples**:
  - High House of Wonders (Baldur's Gate)
  - House of Inspired Hands (Waterdeep, Sea Ward)
  - Maker's Hall (Lantanna)
- **Allies**: Lathander, Milil, Oghma, Waukeen
- **Foes**: Talos (who destroys what Gond creates)
- **Common Followers**: Inventors, blacksmiths, engineers, crafters, tinkers
- **Adventure Hooks**: Dangerous inventions, construct rebellions, stolen blueprints, technology vs nature

### Oghma (The Binder)
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: Knowledge
- **Portfolio**: Knowledge, invention, inspiration, bards
- **Symbol**: Blank scroll
- **Specialty Priests**: Loremasters, Namers
- **Major Temples**:
  - Font of Knowledge (Waterdeep, Castle Ward)
  - House of Oghma (Procampur)
  - Candlekeep (partially dedicated)
- **Allies**: Deneir, Milil, Gond, Mystra
- **Foes**: Cyric, Mask, those who destroy knowledge
- **Common Followers**: Bards, sages, wizards, inventors, artists
- **Adventure Hooks**: Lost libraries, censored knowledge, bardic competitions, stolen songs

### Tempus (The Lord of Battles)
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: War
- **Portfolio**: War, battle, warriors, strategy
- **Symbol**: Flaming silver sword on red field
- **Specialty Priests**: Battleguards
- **Major Temples**:
  - Hall of Heroes (Valkur's Roar, Gundarlun)
  - Abbey of the Sword (near Battledale)
  - Various war memorials
- **Allies**: Red Knight (his daughter), Valkur, Uthgar
- **Foes**: Garagos, Cyric, those who wage war dishonorably
- **Common Followers**: Warriors, soldiers, mercenaries, weapon masters
- **Adventure Hooks**: Honorable combat trials, mercenary contracts, war memorials, strategy challenges

### Waukeen (Merchant's Friend)
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: Knowledge, Trickery
- **Portfolio**: Trade, money, wealth, merchants
- **Symbol**: Gold coin with Waukeen's profile
- **Specialty Priests**: Goldhands
- **Major Temples**:
  - Tower of Gold (Athkatla, Amn)
  - House of Coins (Waterdeep, Southern Ward)
  - Various merchant guildhalls
- **Allies**: Lliira, Gond, Shaundakul, Tymora
- **Foes**: Mask, Cyric, thieves who disrupt trade
- **Common Followers**: Merchants, traders, caravan masters, shopkeepers
- **Adventure Hooks**: Trade route protection, counterfeit coins, merchant guild wars, treasure recovery

## Evil Deities

### Bane (The Black Lord)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Evil
- **Domains**: War, Order (evil)
- **Portfolio**: Tyranny, hatred, fear, strife
- **Symbol**: Black hand, green rays emanating from it
- **Specialty Priests**: Dreadmasters, Dark Imperceptors
- **Major Temples**:
  - Black Lord's Altar (Zhentil Keep - destroyed, rebuilding)
  - Tower of the Black Fist (Mulmaster)
  - Hidden shrines in cities
- **Allies**: Loviatar, Talona, Mask (sometimes)
- **Foes**: Torm, Tyr, Ilmater, Cyric, Mystra, Lathander, all good deities
- **Common Followers**: Tyrants, Zhentarim, dictators, evil fighters
- **Adventure Hooks**: Tyrannical takeovers, Zhentarim plots, fear cults, conquest campaigns

### Bhaal (The Lord of Murder)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Evil
- **Domains**: Death
- **Portfolio**: Murder, violence, assassination
- **Symbol**: Skull surrounded by teardrops
- **Specialty Priests**: Deathstalkers, Slayers
- **Major Temples**:
  - Hidden murder shrines throughout cities
  - Cult of the Dead Three locations
  - Bhaal's former temples (mostly destroyed)
- **Allies**: Bane, Myrkul (Dead Three alliance), Talona, Loviatar
- **Foes**: Lathander, Chauntea, Helm, Tyr, Ilmater
- **Common Followers**: Assassins, serial killers, Bhaalspawn
- **Adventure Hooks**: Assassination plots, Bhaalspawn emergence, ritual murders, death cult resurrection

### Myrkul (Lord of Bones)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Evil
- **Domains**: Death, Grave
- **Portfolio**: Death, decay, exhaustion, the dead, autumn
- **Symbol**: White skull in black triangle
- **Specialty Priests**: Bonemen, Necromantis
- **Major Temples**:
  - Hidden crypts and mausoleums
  - Cult of the Dead Three locations
  - The Bone Castle (extraplanar)
- **Allies**: Bane, Bhaal (Dead Three), Velsharoon, Talona
- **Foes**: Kelemvor, Lathander, Jergal (his former superior), Chauntea
- **Common Followers**: Necromancers, grave robbers, those who fear death
- **Adventure Hooks**: Undead armies, necromancer lairs, death magic corruption, bone relics

### Shar (Mistress of the Night)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Evil
- **Domains**: Death, Trickery
- **Portfolio**: Darkness, night, loss, forgetfulness, secrets, dungeons
- **Symbol**: Black disk with purple border
- **Specialty Priests**: Nightcloaks, Darkmasters
- **Major Temples**:
  - Dark Goddess's Embrace (Calimport, underground)
  - Night Temple (Waterdeep, hidden)
  - Shadowfell strongholds
- **Allies**: Talona, Mask (sometimes)
- **Foes**: Selûne (her twin sister, eternal enemy), Lathander, Mystra, Azuth
- **Common Followers**: Assassins, nihilists, shadow monks, dark wizards
- **Adventure Hooks**: Shadow Weave corruption, Selûne temple attacks, memory theft, Shadowfell incursions

### Talos (The Destroyer)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Evil
- **Domains**: Tempest
- **Portfolio**: Storms, destruction, rebellion, chaos, earthquakes
- **Symbol**: Three lightning bolts radiating from point
- **Specialty Priests**: Stormlords
- **Major Temples**:
  - Storm's Keep (islands off Vilhon Reach)
  - Hidden shrines on mountain peaks
  - Storm-wracked coastlines
- **Allies**: Auril, Umberlee, Malar (the Gods of Fury)
- **Foes**: Chauntea, Lathander, Silvanus, Eldath, civilized deities
- **Common Followers**: Barbarians, pirates, destructive cultists
- **Adventure Hooks**: Unnatural storms, earthquake cults, shipwrecks, nature's wrath

### Cyric (Prince of Lies)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Evil
- **Domains**: Trickery
- **Portfolio**: Lies, intrigue, deception, murder, strife, illusion
- **Symbol**: White jawless skull on purple sunburst
- **Specialty Priests**: Mindbenders
- **Major Temples**:
  - Cult cells (constantly shifting)
  - Hidden shrines masquerading as other faiths
  - The Supreme Throne (extraplanar)
- **Allies**: None (betrays all allies)
- **Foes**: Everyone (especially Mystra, Kelemvor, Mask, all deities)
- **Common Followers**: Madmen, megalomaniacs, liars, those seeking chaos
- **Adventure Hooks**: Imposter cults, lying plagues, false prophecies, madness corruption

### Loviatar (Maiden of Pain)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Evil
- **Domains**: Death
- **Portfolio**: Pain, hurt, agony, torment, suffering, torture
- **Symbol**: Nine-tailed barbed scourge
- **Specialty Priests**: Painmistresses/Painmasters
- **Major Temples**:
  - House of Pain (Calimport)
  - Scarred Altar (Zhentil Keep)
  - Hidden torture chambers
- **Allies**: Bane, Malar, Talona
- **Foes**: Ilmater (her opposite), Eldath, Lliira
- **Common Followers**: Torturers, sadists, those who inflict pain
- **Adventure Hooks**: Torture cults, pain rituals, prisoner interrogations, suffering competitions

## Lesser-Known Deities

### Eldath (Goddess of Singing Waters)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Nature, Peace
- **Portfolio**: Peace, waterfalls, springs, pools, stillness, quiet places
- **Symbol**: Waterfall plunging into still pool
- **Specialty Priests**: Peacemen/Peacewomen
- **Major Temples**:
  - Quiet places by waterfalls and springs
  - Sacred groves with pools
  - Few formal temples (faith prefers nature)
- **Allies**: Silvanus, Mielikki, Chauntea, Selûne
- **Foes**: Malar, Talos, Auril, warlike deities
- **Common Followers**: Pacifists, druids, those seeking peace
- **Adventure Hooks**: Defiled springs, peace negotiations, sanctuary violations, corrupted oases

### Mielikki (Our Lady of the Forest)
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Nature
- **Portfolio**: Forests, forest creatures, rangers, dryads, autumn
- **Symbol**: Unicorn's head
- **Specialty Priests**: Forestarms
- **Major Temples**:
  - Grove of the Unicorn (High Forest)
  - Autumn Hall (various locations)
  - Sacred forest groves
- **Allies**: Silvanus, Eldath, Chauntea, Shiallia
- **Foes**: Malar, Talos, Talona
- **Common Followers**: Rangers, druids, forest dwellers, dryads
- **Adventure Hooks**: Forest protection, ranger missions, dryad rescue, logging disputes

### Mask (Lord of Shadows)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Neutral (leans evil)
- **Domains**: Trickery
- **Portfolio**: Thieves, thievery, shadows, stealth
- **Symbol**: Black velvet mask
- **Specialty Priests**: Demarchesses/Demarchs
- **Major Temples**:
  - Hidden shrines in thieves' guilds
  - Shadow vaults
  - Underground lairs
- **Allies**: Shar (sometimes), Tymora (complicated)
- **Foes**: Cyric, Waukeen, Helm, lawful deities
- **Common Followers**: Thieves, spies, assassins, smugglers
- **Adventure Hooks**: Thieves' guild conflicts, shadow magic, heists, stolen artifacts

### Umberlee (The Bitch Queen)
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Evil
- **Domains**: Tempest
- **Portfolio**: Oceans, currents, waves, sea winds
- **Symbol**: Wave curling left and right
- **Specialty Priests**: Wavemistresses/Wavemasters
- **Major Temples**:
  - The Queen's Keep (Purple Rocks, Sea of Swords)

- **Allies**: Talos, Auril, Malar (Gods of Fury)
- **Foes**: Selûne, Valkur, Sune, peaceful sea deities
- **Common Followers**: Pirates, sailors (reluctant), coastal raiders
- **Adventure Hooks**: Shipwrecks, sea monster attacks, pirate curses, drowning sacrifices

### Tyr (The Even-Handed)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Domains**: War, Order
- **Portfolio**: Justice, law, fairness
- **Symbol**: Balanced scales on warhammer
- **Specialty Priests**: Hammers of Tyr
- **Major Temples**:
  - Halls of Justice (many cities)
  - Court of All Justice (Procampur)
  - City courthouse connections
- **Allies**: Torm, Ilmater, Lathander, Helm
- **Foes**: Bane, Cyric, Mask, all unjust deities
- **Common Followers**: Judges, paladins, lawyers, city guards
- **Adventure Hooks**: Courtroom dramas, corruption exposure, unjust laws, justice missions

### Beshaba (Maid of Misfortune)

- **Domains**: Trickery

- **Foes**: Tymora (her opposite twin), Lathander, good fortune deities
- **Common Followers**: Unlucky, cursed, those who spread misfortune
- **Adventure Hooks**: Curse removal, bad luck epidemics, gambling sabotage, hex investigation

### Helm (The Vigilant One)
- **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral
- **Domains**: Life, Light, Twilight

- **Symbol**: Staring eye on upright gauntlet

### Azuth (The High One)

### Talona (Lady of Poison)

- **Common Followers**: Plague spreaders, poisoners, disease cultists

## Using Deity Information in Adventures

### Quick Selection Guide

**For nature adventures**: Chauntea, Silvanus, Mielikki, Eldath, Malar
**For city intrigue**: Tymora, Mask, Waukeen, Bane, Cyric
**For undead threats**: Kelemvor vs. Myrkul, Lathander, Jergal
**For naval/coastal**: Umberlee, Valkur, Selûne, Talos
**For war/combat**: Tempus, Torm, Tyr, Bane, Red Knight
**For magic**: Mystra, Azuth, Savras, Shar (Shadow Weave)

### Temple as Adventure Location

Temples provide instant adventure sites:
- **Corrupted temple**: Evil forces have taken over
- **Lost temple**: Ancient ruin to explore
- **Rival temples**: Conflicting faiths clash
- **Sacred relic**: Mcguffin tied to deity

### Faction Rewards with Deities

When adventures align with a deity's portfolio:
- **Renown**: Gain standing with that faith's order
- **Blessings**: Temporary divine favor (Charm of [Deity])
- **Holy items**: Blessed water, symbols, minor relics
- **Clergy contacts**: NPCs for future adventures

### NPC Priests

Use specialty priest titles for authentic NPCs:
- "Dawnbringer Kael of Lathander" (not just "Priest Kael")
- "Luckbringer Mira serves Tymora" (not "Cleric Mira")
- "The Battleguard stood watch" (Tempus follower)
