# Faerun Lore Reference

Quick reference for creating authentic Faerun adventures. This is a curated subset of the most useful lore for one-page adventures.

## Major Cities and Regions

### Sword Coast
- **Waterdeep**: City of Splendors, ruled by Lords of Waterdeep (masked nobles), major trade hub
- **Neverwinter**: "Jewel of the North," recovering from cataclysm, Lord Neverember rules
- **Baldur's Gate**: Merchant city, morally gray, entrance to Avernus
- **Luskan**: Pirate city ruled by Ship Captains, lawless and dangerous

### Other Major Locations
- **Candlekeep**: Fortress library, requires book donation for entry, monks and scholars
- **Undermountain**: Massive dungeon beneath Waterdeep, created by Halaster the Mad Mage
- **Icewind Dale**: Frozen north, Ten-Towns, dangerous wilderness
- **Calimport**: Desert city in the south, thieves' guilds and genies

### Smaller Locations (Great for Adventures)
- **Phandalin**: Small frontier town, near ruins and mines
- **Red Larch**: Farming community, mysterious underground cults
- **Triboar**: Trade town at crossroads, frequent monster attacks
- **Secomber**: Small town on Delimbiyr River, frontier outpost

## Factions

### Major Factions (Ideal for Adventure Hooks)

**Harpers**
- Symbol: Harp and moon
- Goals: Balance, oppose tyranny, preserve knowledge
- Secretive network of spies and agents
- Good for: Espionage, protecting innocents, stopping evil plots

**Lords' Alliance**
- Coalition of rulers from major cities
- Goals: Mutual defense, trade, stability
- Members: Lords of Waterdeep, Neverwinter, Silverymoon, etc.
- Good for: Political intrigue, city defense, diplomatic missions

**Order of the Gauntlet**
- Symbol: Gauntlet
- Goals: Destroy evil, protect the weak
- Paladins and clerics of good deities (Torm, Tyr, Helm)
- Good for: Undead hunting, fighting fiends, righteous quests

**Emerald Enclave**
- Druids and rangers preserving natural balance
- Goals: Protect wilderness, restore nature
- Good for: Nature corruption, aberrations, elemental threats

**Zhentarim (Black Network)**
- Symbol: Winged snake
- Goals: Wealth, power, influence (not necessarily evil)
- Mercenaries, merchants, spies
- Good for: Morally gray jobs, smuggling, power struggles

### Villain Factions

**Cult of the Dragon**
- Seeks to transform dragons into dracoliches
- Worships Tiamat
- Good for: Dragon threats, undead dragons

**Red Wizards of Thay**
- Evil wizards from Thay
- Seek magical power and conquest
- Good for: Necromancy, magical experiments, planar threats

**Xanathar Guild**
- Criminal organization in Waterdeep
- Led by a paranoid beholder (Xanathar)
- Good for: Urban crime, kidnapping, treasure heists

## Using This Reference

When creating adventures:
1. Pick a **location** (city or region)
2. Choose a **faction** for the hook (or villain)
3. Select a **deity** for thematic connection
4. Add **cultural flavor** (expressions, seasons, local customs)
5. Use **appropriate monsters** for the region

Example: Adventure in Red Larch (location) involving Emerald Enclave (faction) stopping blight caused by Silvanus cult gone wrong (deity), featuring corrupted awakened trees (monsters) during Leaffall (season).
